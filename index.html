About Me

Since childhood, I have had a strong interest in the world of art – from drawing, visual design, to dance. This talent and interest have continued to evolve over time, shaping my imaginative perspective and sensitivity to aesthetics. However, I also have a high curiosity about how the world works, which then led me to choose an educational path in engineering. In the world of engineering, I found space to develop logical thinking and problem-solving skills. Interestingly, my background in art has actually become an advantage – I am more sensitive to detail, able to think creatively, and enjoy unconventional approaches to problem-solving.I believe that the combination of art and engineering is not two opposing things, but rather two sides of a complete creative process. My vision is to integrate both to create works that are not only functionally effective but also hold artistic and humanistic value.
